Home Page

Welcome to our webpage showcasing our investigation into the current smartphone illiteracy of the elderly, researched by Sarah Manikam, Shannon Ma and Rochelle Mitchell

The topic we picked, 'The Elderly's Current Smartphone Illiteracy,' was one we believed would be intruguing, relevant and important. This group of individuals has a history of facing trouble interacting with what the younger generations would consider simple technology. Given the increased use of smartphones in daily life, we were especially interested in researching their interactions with smartphones.
