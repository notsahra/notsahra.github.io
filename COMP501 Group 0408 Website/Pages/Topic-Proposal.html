Topic Description

The topic we picked, "the elderly's current smartphone illiteracy," was one we believed would be intriguing, relevant, and important. This group have a history of facing trouble interacting with what we consider simple technology and given the increased use of smartphones in daily life, we were especially interested in researching their interaction with smartphones.
